About the Project

Danganronpa: Friends of Friends is a fan-made reimagining of the Danganronpa concept, created as a small, non-commercial fan project in the UK. This story features a new cast of characters and plenty of despair, written for entertainment and freely accessible to all fans.

Content Warning!!!

This story contains themes of violence, psychological distress, extreme language and mature content that may not be suitable for all audiences. Reader discretion is advised.
